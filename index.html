About me

Hi my name is Joseph Holder. I’ve been tinkering with toys and electronics since I was a kid. One of my greatest accomplishments to date, occurred in elementary school. I adapted my remote control car so it could drive in the snow. That was a fun winter in the Poconos.

As I grew up my my curiosity in how things work has grown with me. Now I am interested in how computers and the ever-expanding internet work. To help satisfy this interest I have enrolled in Penn University’s Coding Bootcamp. I have already learned a lot but I know we have barely scraped the surface. I cannot wait to learn more.
